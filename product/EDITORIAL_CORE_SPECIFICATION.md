# Editorial Core Specification v1.0

## Invariants

1. Planning precedes writing.
2. Evidence planning precedes factual drafting.
3. Coverage is bounded by reader intent.
4. Article completion means publication approval, not text generation.
5. Blocking safety failures cannot be averaged away.

## Engine sequence

EC01 Input Validation
EC02 Keyword Intelligence
EC03 Search Intent Intelligence
EC04 Reader Intelligence
EC05 Editorial Brief
EC06 Coverage Planning
EC07 Reader Journey
EC08 Evidence Planning
EC09 Structure Design
EC10 Content Generation
EC11 Editorial Review
EC12 Publication Readiness

Each engine emits a structured record consumed by the next engine and retained in the final audit trail.
