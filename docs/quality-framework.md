# New Article Quality Framework v1.0

## Quality dimensions

| Dimension | Purpose | Minimum |
|---|---|---:|
| Search intent | Align answer with the dominant user need | 80 |
| Reader value | Solve the reader's problem and next question | 80 |
| Coverage | Include necessary topics without padding | 80 |
| Evidence | Support factual, numerical and time-sensitive claims | 85 |
| Readability | Natural Japanese and accessible explanations | 80 |
| Originality | Provide a clear editorial angle and added value | 75 |
| SEO | Clear title, structure and semantic relevance | 80 |
| Safety | Avoid fabrication and unsafe assertions | 100 for blocking checks |
| Publication readiness | Overall approval decision | 80 |

## Overall decision

- PASS: all blocking checks pass and total score is 85 or higher.
- PASS_WITH_WARNING: no blocking failure, total score 80 or higher, minor warnings remain.
- NEEDS_REVISION: editorial or coverage defects prevent publication.
- NEEDS_EVIDENCE: one or more material claims lack adequate support.
- NEEDS_EXPERT_REVIEW: YMYL or specialist judgment is required.
- BLOCKED: fabrication, unsafe instruction, unsupported high-risk claim or contract failure.

A high average score never overrides a blocking failure.
