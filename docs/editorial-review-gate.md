# Editorial Review Gate

## Review lenses

### Reader lens
- The answer appears early.
- Prerequisites and terminology are understandable.
- The article resolves the primary problem.
- The next likely question is handled or linked.

### Editorial lens
- No redundant sections or repeated conclusions.
- Headings represent distinct reader questions.
- Transitions are natural.
- FAQ adds unresolved value rather than duplicating the body.

### SEO lens
- The main keyword is represented naturally.
- Title and introduction match the same intent.
- Coverage is semantically complete without keyword stuffing.

### Commercial lens
- Monetization does not distort the conclusion.
- CTA timing follows reader readiness.
- Disclosure requirements are preserved.

## Output

The review produces defects, warnings, recommended revisions and a publication decision.
