# Coverage Engine

## Purpose

The Coverage Engine determines what must be explained for the selected intent and article type. It prevents both missing essential topics and unnecessary encyclopedic expansion.

## Coverage classes

- MUST: required to satisfy the primary intent
- SHOULD: valuable for confidence, execution or comparison
- MAY: optional depth for a specific audience
- EXCLUDE: out of scope or better handled by another article

The output is a Coverage Map with rationale, section destination and evidence requirement.
